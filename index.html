About Us

Welcome to our restaurant! We are proud to provide a unique and distinctive dining experience.

Al-Nubalaa Restaurant combines the heritage and culture of Eastern cuisine with the modernity of Western cuisine in its distinctive meals and desserts, and conveys the Levantine atmosphere at parties, holidays and events.

Our restaurant was established in 2005, and since then, we have been striving to provide the best food and service to our guests. Our vision is to be the preferred destination for lovers of fine dining and delicious cuisine.
